The Avionics program has a CTSO (Career Technical Student Organization) called SkillsUSA. The students learn leadership skills through this program and they will partake in two SkillsUSA Fall Conferences, two SkillsUSA State Conferences, and two SkillsUSA National Conferences throughout their 2 years in this program.
