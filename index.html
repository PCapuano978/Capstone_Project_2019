About Us

Avionics / Drone Electronics is a program spanning two years that is expertly designed to educate students on electronics in relation to avionics. Students will greatly enhance their knowledge on installation, troubleshooting, system integration and avionics operating systems, including GPS systems, autopilot, communication systems, and weather radar when taking this program.

West-MEC prepares students to seek one or more industry certification or licensure in their field of study, providing a pathway to economic independence.
