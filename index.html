About Us

Avionics / Drone Electronics is a program spanning two years that is designed to educate students on electronics within the aviation industry. Students will learn avionics installation, troubleshooting, system integration and avionics operating systems, including GPS systems, autopilot, communication systems, and weather radar.

West-MEC programs prepare students to seek one or more industry certification or licensure in their field of study, providing a pathway to economic independence.
